Economy

The primary goal is to make the Vidarbha region 1 Trillion Dollar GSDP, this will add a good amount to India’s Economy. Creating Job Opportunities, increasing industrial zones and improving services.

Agriculture

Revolutionising Agriculture and Farming is one of the major points to work on. Encouraging supplementary sectors of agriculture, improving standards of farmers and developing agriculture based industries.

Infrastructure

Revamping Infrastructure will be the backbone and give a boost to growth and development of the region. Improved connectivity of rural areas with urban regions and ease of access will play major role.

Healthcare

Healthcare is a very important part of life and improving healthcare sector is the number one priority. A healthy population will indirectly boost the economy of the region as rightly said ‘Health is Wealth’.

Education

Quality Education can improve the population’s way of life, it increases skills and gives a better opportunity for jobs. Increasing number of schools, colleges and universities will have a significant impact.

Climate

Uneven Development can cause grave damage to the environment. Climate Change is a serious challenge and giving back to nature is a need of time. Development will be sustainable and green.
